Mac and Cheese Recipe

Mac and cheese is a simple dish that contains macaroni pasta with cheese. Although simple at first, there are many complexities in making good mac and cheese. A soul food for many being a simple yet delicious dish that can be prepared simply.

Ingredients

  • 1 box of macaroni
  • 1/4 cup of butter
  • 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • ground black pepper
  • 2 cups of milk
  • 2 cups of shredded Chedder cheese

Instructions

  1. Boil macaroni until cooked but still firm
  2. Melt butter in a saucepan
  3. Add flour,salt and pepper, stir until smooth
  4. Slowly pour in milk while stiring, cook until mixture is smooth and bubbling
  5. Add chedder cheese and stir until melted
  6. Drain macaoroni and foled into cheese sauce
  7. Serve and enjoy!
