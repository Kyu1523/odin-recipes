Pb&J Recipe

A simple yet delicious meal that anyone can make and enjoy. A PB&J is a sandwich that contains peanut butter and jam/jelly. The smooth sweet peanut butter is complimented with the sweeter brighter jelly that fuse into a sweet delicious sandwich.

Ingredients

  • Toast
  • Peanut Butter
  • Jelly/Jam

Instructions

  1. Take the toast and spread peanut butter on one side
  2. Then on the other side spread jam or jelly
  3. Close the sandwich together and serve
